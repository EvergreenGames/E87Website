About Engineering


Engineering by definition is "the branch of science and technology concerned with the design, building, and use of engines, machines, and structures"

More broadly, engineering is about helping others.

Engineering is about innovating and creating solutions that are useful to other people.

Engineers apply math, science, and technology to solve problems faced by others.


About CS


CS, or Computer Science, is the study of computers and computation systems.

Computer Science mostly deals with software, such as programming, which is different from Electrical Engineering for example, which deals mostly with hardware.

In Computer Science, there are many key areas of focus, such as:

  1. Artificial Intelligence:

    Artificial Intelligence, or Machine Intelligence, is the simulation of human intelligence and human action.
    AI is a large, growing field in technology.
  2. Cybersecurity
  3. Computer Systems and Networks
  4. Vision and Graphics
  5. Database Systems
  6. And much more!
