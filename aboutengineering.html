About Engineering


Engineering by definition is "the branch of science and technology concerned with the design, building, and use of engines, machines, and structures"

More broadly, engineering is about helping others.

Engineering is about innovating and creating solutions that are useful to other people.

Engineers apply math, science, and technology to solve problems faced by others.

Here are some examples of types of engineering:

  1. Environmental Engineering:
    •Involves the study and research of how to improve our environment as well as the impact humans have on the environment.
    •Considered a subset of civil engineering.
  2. Agricultural Engineering:
    •Applies engineering discipline to the agricultural industry.
    •Topics of study include soil conservation, irrigation, farm machine design, ground preparation, and more.
  3. Sustainability Engineering:
    •There are three major categories of sustainability: Environmental impact, social consideration, and economic consideration.
    •Work after getting the degree may include hazardous waste management, emission control, wastewater management, and more.
